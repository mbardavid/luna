## Validation guidance for next capture pass
For each future concrete target, record:
- Handle/account name
- Segment
- Why the account fits at least one approved content theme
- Example post or thread that demonstrates signal quality
- Tone/risk note including any hype, hostility, or low-trust behavior
- Whether the account is approved for watch-only, reply-consideration, or exclude

## Exclusion reminder
Do not add targets solely because they are large, viral, or adjacent to growth loops. Fit, evidence quality, and tone safety are mandatory.
