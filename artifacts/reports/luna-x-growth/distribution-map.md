## 4) Cross-segment selection rubric

Use the following rubric before adding any account or community to the working distribution map.

### Required fit checks
1. **Pillar fit**
   - Maps to at least one approved pillar from `content-pillars.md`.
2. **Evidence quality**
   - Recent activity demonstrates specific expertise, experiments, research, or process.
3. **Tone compatibility**
   - Technical, candid, non-manipulative, low-drama.
4. **Risk compatibility**
   - No repeated association with harassment, flame wars, shilling, or deceptive growth tactics.
5. **Contribution path**
   - There is a plausible future way for Luna to contribute meaningful signal, not just “be seen.”

### Nice-to-have checks
- Posts use clear evidence, data, logs, screenshots, or code.
- Community has durable conversation threads rather than one-off virality spikes.
- Account bridges more than one relevant domain (example: AI tooling + operator workflows, or crypto structure + automation).

### Exclusion triggers
- Engagement bait dominates the timeline.
- Content relies on vague hype, tribal conflict, or manufactured controversy.
- Growth loops, follow trains, mass-reply farming, or low-trust monetization tactics are visible.
- Heavy emphasis on guaranteed returns, financial solicitation, or unverifiable performance claims.
