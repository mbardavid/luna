# M1 Metrics Tracker Template

Status: Draft template  
Scope: internal measurement only  
Mode: planning/docs only — no public actions on X

## Usage notes
- Use one daily row per operating day.
- Fill post-level rows only for approved/published content after human go-live approval.
- Keep risk and approval columns mandatory even when no post is made.
- Weekly rollup should summarize quality, not only raw counts.

## Daily qualitative fields
- **Follower quality sample:** list sampled new followers and classify by segment fit, signal quality, and any spam/bot indicators.
- **Risk flags:** note tone drift, unsupported claims, off-pillar content pressure, suspicious engagement, or policy concerns.
- **Approval status:** confirm whether the day remained planning-only, or whether any item passed human approval.

## Weekly review prompts
1. Did new followers match approved segments from the distribution map?
2. Which theme/format produced the best fit-adjusted result?
3. Were any posts held or rejected for safety/governance reasons?
4. Did engagement quality improve, degrade, or stay noisy/unclear?
5. Should the next week continue, adjust, or pause?
