# Luna X Growth Canary Charter

Status: active draft for Autonomy canary
Updated: 2026-03-06

## Objective

Use Luna's existing X account as the first externally validated autonomy project.
The operating goal is to grow the account by net new followers while preserving trust,
clarity, and alignment with OpenClaw's crypto market intelligence mission.

Primary milestones:
- M1: +25 net followers from day-0 baseline
- M2: +200 net followers from day-0 baseline

## Account Positioning

The account is not a generic growth sandbox.
It represents a real public identity connected to OpenClaw.

The account should stay inside these pillars:
- crypto market structure and microstructure insights
- build in public about autonomous research/execution systems
- data-backed observations, experiments, and lessons learned
- thoughtful reactions to live market developments when evidence exists

## Allowed Without Chairman Approval

- posts and replies that stay inside the approved pillars
- tests of timing, hook, formatting, CTA, and packaging
- routine daily publishing and engagement work
- iteration on content calendar and experiment mix

## Prohibited Without Chairman Approval

- changing the account's public identity or macro positioning
- explicit financial advice or promises of return
- political partisanship, personal attacks, flame wars, or harassment
- paid growth hacks, bought followers, follow-unfollow loops, giveaway spam
- outbound DM campaigns or anything that looks like unsolicited solicitation
- publishing claims about trades or profits without verifiable evidence

## Steering Triggers

Escalate to Chairman when any of the following happens:
- major audience pivot or persona change
- negative public reaction beyond normal disagreement
- experiments that look spammy, misleading, or reputationally risky
- monetization attempt, public offer, or other real-business action
- any suggestion to leave the approved content pillars

## Daily Operating Loop

Every daily cycle must produce:
- session health snapshot
- profile snapshot with follower delta vs baseline
- scorecard for the last 24h
- ranked hypotheses: winner / neutral / loser
- recommendation: continue / adjust / pause / steering
- updated board packet

Canonical artifact paths:
- `artifacts/reports/luna-x-growth/session-health-latest.json`
- `artifacts/reports/luna-x-growth/profile-snapshot-latest.json`
